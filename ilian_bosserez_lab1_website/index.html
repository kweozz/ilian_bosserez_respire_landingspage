About us

Ilian Bosserez, inspired by the creativity of Instagram closet pages and the treasure hunt of Vinted and Pinterest, envisioned a platform that empowers self-expression through sustainable fashion. Respire was born!

We believe fashion should be fun, expressive, and kind to the planet. Respire is a community where you can find inspiration, connect with like-minded individuals, and shop for pre-loved clothing that reflects your unique style.
